Research

I’m currently a Ph.D. candidate at Rutgers, and my advisor is Alyson Brooks. I’m also a member of the N-body shop collaboration. My research interests include dwarf galaxies, cosmological simulations, star formation, and more recently, machine learning.

Simulations

I most closely work with the simulations from the N-body shop, particularly the MARVEL-ous dwarfs and the DC Justice League.
